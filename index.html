What is Attack on Titan?


Attack on Titan is a critically acclaimed anime series that follows humanity's desperate struggle for survival against gigantic creatures known as Titans. What begins as a story of action and revenge gradually unfolds into a powerful tale of war, freedom, sacrifice, and the consequences of hatred, making it one of the most thought-provoking anime of all time.

Why do I suggest every teenager to watch Attack on Titan?


I suggest every teenager watch Attack on Titan because it is more than just an action anime. Through its intense story, unforgettable characters, and powerful themes, it explores freedom, responsibility, sacrifice, and the consequences of hatred and war. It challenges viewers to think critically about the world around them while delivering one of the most engaging and emotional stories ever created.

Who is my fav. main character in Attack on Titan?


My favorite character in Attack on Titan is Eren Yeager. What makes him so compelling is his unwavering determination to pursue freedom, no matter the cost. Throughout the series, his journey evolves from that of a passionate and impulsive boy to one of the most complex and thought-provoking characters in anime, making him both inspiring and unforgettable.

Who is my fav. female character in Attack on Titan?


My favorite female characters in Attack on Titan are Mikasa Ackerman and Hange Zoë. Mikasa stands out for her incredible strength, loyalty, and determination, while Hange captivates me with her intelligence, curiosity, and unique personality. Together, they represent two very different but equally inspiring sides of the series, making them some of its most memorable characters.
